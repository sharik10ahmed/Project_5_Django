Refund Policy

1. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds for items returned within 30 days of purchase in their original condition. Items must be:

  • Unused and in original packaging
  • With all accessories and documentation
  • With the original receipt or order confirmation
  • Not damaged due to customer misuse

2. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refund:

  • Clearance or sale items
  • Digital products or downloads
  • Custom or personalized orders
  • Items showing signs of wear or damage

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Log in to your PickUp account
  2. Go to "My Orders"
  3. Select the order and click "Return Item"
  4. Fill in the return form with reason
  5. Print the shipping label and send the item back

4. Inspection and Processing

Once we receive your return, we will inspect the item within 5-7 business days. If approved, the refund will be processed within 7-10 business days to your original payment method.

5. Refund Amount

The refund amount will include the product price. Original shipping charges are non-refundable. Return shipping charges are covered by PickUp if the product is defective.

6. Defective Products

If you receive a defective or damaged product, contact us immediately. We will provide a replacement or full refund at no cost, including return shipping.

7. Partial Refunds

If items have been used or are damaged beyond normal wear and tear, we may offer a partial refund at our discretion.

8. Contact Us

For refund inquiries, please contact our support team at support@pickup.com or call 1-800-PICKUP.
