Shipping Policy

1. Shipping Methods

We offer multiple shipping options to meet your needs:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day delivery
  • Free Shipping: On orders above ₹500

2. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on:

  • Delivery location (urban/remote)
  • Package weight and dimensions
  • Selected shipping method
  • Order value

3. Delivery Areas

We deliver across India to:

  • All major cities and towns
  • Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities
  • Select remote areas (with additional charges)

4. Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Processing time excludes weekends and public holidays. You will receive a tracking number once your order ships.

5. Tracking Your Order

You can track your shipment in real-time through:

  • Your PickUp account dashboard
  • Email notifications
  • SMS updates
  • Carrier website using tracking number

6. Delivery Signature

Delivery to your specified address may require a signature. Please ensure someone is available to receive the package.

7. Lost or Damaged Shipments

If your shipment is lost or damaged during transit, contact us immediately with:

  • Order number and tracking number
  • Photos of damaged packaging (if applicable)
  • Proof of delivery issue

We will investigate and provide a replacement or refund.

8. International Shipping

International shipping is available for select products. Please contact us for rates and delivery times.

9. Contact Us

For shipping inquiries, email us at shipping@pickup.com or call 1-800-PICKUP.
